Description: Our hearts beat for weddings like this. It's always nice to end a long day shooting in the Irish countryside by the fire with some good wine and our host's dog Sandy curled up at our feet. Thank you Michael and Allison for letting us be a part of your day, and thank you for having it in such a beautiful place.

From the couple:
Ever since that day when we tied the knot back in March 2013 in Co. Kerry, Ireland we'd been counting down to the moment when Tim would send us the Wedding Video. It's probably the only chance we had to relive the day and see what everyone else was seeing while we were smiling constantly all day for photographs, sitting through speeches, cutting (then eating) cake and dancing with every pair of eyes in the room watching... It's an exhausting day for those getting married and it passes by so quickly. Our film never gets old. The music chosen really works with the images captured to make that special day feel even more captivating. We're so happy we had  Velare document one of the happiest days in our lives.
